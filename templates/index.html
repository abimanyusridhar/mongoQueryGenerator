What is DataSpeak?

DataSpeak is an NLP-driven system designed to interact with NoSQL databases like MongoDB. It deciphers your database collections, extracts schema-like insights, and translates natural language queries into efficient MongoDB queries, enabling intuitive data exploration and management.
